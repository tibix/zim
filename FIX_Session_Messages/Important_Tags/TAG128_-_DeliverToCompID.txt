====== TAG128 - DeliverToCompID ======
Created Wednesday 08 June 2016

Assigned value used to identify the firm targeted to receive the message if the message is delivered by a third party   i.e.  the third party firm identifier would be delivered in the TargetCompID ([[TAG56 - TargetCompID|56]]) field and the ultimate receiver firm ID in this field.
