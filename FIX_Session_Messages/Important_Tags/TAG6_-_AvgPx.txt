====== TAG6 - AvgPx ======
Created Wednesday 08 June 2016

Calculated average price of all fills on this order. 

For Fixed Income trades AvgPx is always expressed as percent-of-par, regardless of the PriceType (423) of LastPx ([[TAG31 - LastPx|31]]).  I.e., AvgPx will contain an average of percent-of-par values (see LastParPx (669)) for issues traded in Yield, Spread or Discount.
